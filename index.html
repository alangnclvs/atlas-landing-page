Meet Atlas

Atlas is a humanoid robot developed by Boston Dynamics. It is designed for a variety of search and rescue tasks, as well as industrial applications.

With its advanced sensors and mobility capabilities, Atlas can navigate through rough terrain, climb stairs, and manipulate objects with its dexterous hands.

Key Features

Agile Mobility

Atlas is equipped with advanced sensors and actuators that enable agile and dynamic movements, allowing it to navigate challenging environments with ease.

Dexterous Manipulation

Atlas features highly dexterous hands with a wide range of motion and tactile feedback, enabling it to grasp and manipulate objects with precision.

Advanced Vision System

With its advanced vision system, Atlas can perceive and interpret its surroundings in real-time, allowing it to autonomously navigate and interact with the environment.
